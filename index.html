I am a PhD researcher at LMU Munich, in the Institute of AI in Management (Feuerriegel Group) and affiliated with the Munich Center for Machine Learning (MCML). My research is in causal machine learning — semiparametric estimation, off-policy evaluation, and policy learning — with a growing focus on applications to LLM alignment and safety.
